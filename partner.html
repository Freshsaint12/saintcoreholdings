Partnerships

We keep partnerships small and long.

Saint Core Holdings doesn't take outside capital, but we do work closely with a short list of firms, schools, operators, and long-horizon investors. If one of the paths below matches, we'd like to hear from you.

Who we partner with.

We don't try to serve everyone. These are the conversations that usually go somewhere:

  • Firms Tax and financial services firms adopting or evaluating SaintCoreTax.
  • Schools Nursing programs and exam-prep organizations partnering with StudyNovia.
  • Operators Builders and operators with deep category experience in one of our verticals.
  • Investors Long-horizon investors who want to understand the company — not raise a round.
  • Press Journalists covering professional software, AI in regulated industries, or Chicago tech.

What we're not looking for.

Outbound agency pitches, SaaS "partnership" cold emails, outside funding, and white-label reseller proposals. If that's the conversation, we'll save both of us the time.
