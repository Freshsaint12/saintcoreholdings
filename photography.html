Meet The Artist

Capturing Life's Beautiful Moments

With over a decade behind the lens, I've dedicated my career to capturing the authentic moments that make life extraordinary. From intimate elopements to corporate events, my approach combines technical expertise with an artistic eye for storytelling.

Based in Chicago, I believe every photograph should evoke emotion and tell a story. My style blends classic elegance with contemporary vision, creating timeless images that you'll treasure for generations.

When I'm not shooting, you'll find me exploring Chicago's architecture, mentoring aspiring photographers, or planning my next adventure. I'd love to hear about your vision and bring it to life.

— The Saint Core Team
