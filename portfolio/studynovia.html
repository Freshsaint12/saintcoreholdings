StudyNovia

An adaptive NCLEX preparation platform that rebuilds tomorrow's study plan from last night's answers. Designed for the way nursing students actually study — on the bus, before rounds, at 2 a.m.

Visit StudyNovia.com School & program partnerships

The problem

NCLEX preparation is long, lonely, and built on question banks that don't adapt to what the student actually missed. Most prep products are large PDFs with a quiz engine on top. Students memorize rationales they won't remember the next morning and blame themselves when it doesn't stick.

The approach

StudyNovia reads the last fifty answers, identifies the concepts that are slipping, and rebuilds tomorrow's study plan around them. The platform is mobile-first because nursing students aren't on desktops — they're on buses, in breakrooms, and between clinicals.

What's inside

  • Adaptive question engine. Every session tunes the next one. The weak concepts come back sooner; the mastered ones back off.
  • StudyPods. Podcast-format audio lessons built for the commute, the walk, the workout. Listen like a podcast, learn like a course.
  • Novia. An AI study companion that explains the rationale in plain English, not textbook voice, and remembers what you've already worked through.
  • Performance insights. Real-time readiness against the NCLEX blueprint — not a vanity score.

Who it's for

Nursing students preparing for the NCLEX — first-time test-takers, re-takers, and the programs that support them.

Status

Live in production. Visit studynovia.com to sign up. For nursing school or program partnerships, use the partner form.
