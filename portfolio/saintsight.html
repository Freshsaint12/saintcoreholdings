SaintSight

See it before the world does. A global early-opportunity intelligence platform tracking emerging trends, businesses, and investments across seven world regions and more than 190 countries — with the guidance to actually act on them.

Join the early-access list Back to portfolio

The premise

The biggest financial wins in life don't go to the smartest people. They go to the earliest. Bitcoin, Amazon, YouTube, moringa farming in Ghana — every one of them was obvious in hindsight. SaintSight exists to make them obvious before the world catches on.

The problem

Billions of people sit inches away from life-changing opportunities every day and have no way to see them. A Ghanaian professional in Chicago has Western income and deep ties in West Africa, but no structured intelligence about which agricultural exports are gaining global demand. A first-generation graduate wants to build wealth but doesn't know which certifications are exploding or which business models are still early.

Existing platforms either serve billion-dollar institutions at prices that exclude individuals, or they track trends for SEO professionals — not for people trying to build wealth. Not one platform combines global early-signal detection with practical entry guidance and step-by-step execution for individuals.

The approach — three pillars

01 · Signal Tracker

An automated intelligence engine pulls from dozens of global sources every four hours — GitHub, Google Trends, African Development Bank data, ECOWAS trade statistics, crypto activity, startup funding databases, job market reports, patent filings, and more. Every signal receives a proprietary Earliness Score (0–100) that tells users exactly how early they are in any given opportunity.

  • Geographic Earliness Score. How early the opportunity is in each country — not just globally.
  • Trend Migration. Where an opportunity is in its geographic journey — e.g., whether a Korean trend is 18 months from hitting the US.
  • Morning Brief. Personalized daily brief at 7 a.m.
  • Global Tab. Signals filtered to only the countries each user cares about.
  • Window Closing Alerts. Real-time notifications when an Earliness Score begins to drop.

02 · Opportunity Library

A structured, searchable library of every proven path to a better financial life — organized across ten categories: businesses to start, certifications, education, careers, import/export, diaspora-specific plays, investments, real estate, franchise/licensing, and commission-based paths.

Education and certification entries include real labor-market intelligence — salary by career level, job-posting growth, unfilled positions, six-month employment rates, and a SaintSight Outlook rating. Searchable by country, capital required, time to income, and demand status.

03 · Journey System

Information without execution is worthless. When a user decides to pursue a signal or opportunity, SaintSight generates a phased, step-by-step Journey Checklist — from first week to first revenue. Each step includes why it matters, how to complete it, what to expect, and a realistic time estimate.

Users can run multiple journeys in parallel, sync progress across devices, set milestones, and receive intelligent reminders. SaintSight AI — a proprietary intelligence assistant — answers questions tailored to the exact signal, path, and step each user is on.

Global coverage

Seven world regions with dedicated signal sourcing: North America, Africa (with unique signal infrastructure across Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ethiopia, Tanzania, and more), Asia, Europe, Latin America, the Middle East, and global cross-region. Users pick which countries appear on their Global Tab.

The diaspora focus

SaintSight has a deliberate focus on the African diaspora — over 50 million people with Western incomes and deep family connections back home, sending hundreds of billions in remittances each year with virtually no structured investment intelligence. The platform speaks directly to that need, surfacing signals — like moringa processing from Ghana under AGOA — with the context and execution guidance to act on them.

Availability & pricing

  • Free browse. Signal feed, Earliness Scores, and Opportunity Library without an account.
  • Free account. Limited daily access to signal deep dives and the morning brief.
  • PRO — $19.99/month. Unlimited signals, entry playbooks, journey checklists, SaintSight AI, alerts, and the full library.
  • ELITE — $49.99/month. Insider Feed, VC and crypto pre-listing alerts, and priority intelligence.

Five launch languages: English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Swahili — reaching over two billion people across the regions SaintSight serves.
