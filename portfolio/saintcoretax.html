SaintCoreTax

Eight modules in one login. The practice operating system for tax and financial services firms that want one platform, not eleven browser tabs.

Visit SaintCoreTax.com Firm partnerships

The problem

A modern tax firm runs on eleven tools that don't talk: an intake form, a signature service, a scanner, a billing platform, a storage drive, a shared inbox, a project board, a CRM, a portal, a website, and a spreadsheet everyone pretends isn't the source of truth. By April, nobody can find anything and the clients have noticed.

The approach

SaintCoreTax is a single platform that does the job of that entire stack — designed specifically for the way tax and financial services firms actually work, not as a generic CRM with a tax skin.

What's inside

Eight integrated modules, all sharing the same client record, the same document vault, and the same billing and compliance primitives:

  • Client intake. Configurable forms, e-signature, and ID upload — the front door to every engagement.
  • Document scanning. AI-assisted extraction for W-2s, 1099s, K-1s, and receipts.
  • E-signature & engagement letters. First-party, no DocuSign surcharge.
  • Client portal. The portal your partners will actually send clients to.
  • Billing & payments. Invoices, retainers, and Stripe out of the box.
  • Workflow & tasks. Status that reflects the real state of a return, not a checkbox nobody updates.
  • Messaging. In-product chat plus WhatsApp integration for firms that already live there.
  • Admin & compliance. Roles, audit trails, retention — the parts regulators care about.

Who it's for

Small and mid-size tax and financial services firms who feel the weight of running ten tools and want to feel the freedom of running one.

Status

Live in production. Built, hosted, and supported by Saint Core Holdings. For firm onboarding, visit saintcoretax.com directly.
