Photography & Studio

4 Questions
What equipment is included with studio rental? +
Our 2,500 sq ft studio features 18 ft ceilings, a full cyclorama wall, Profoto and Arri lighting equipment, and ample space for creative productions. Additional equipment can be arranged upon request.
Do you offer photography services or just studio rental? +
We offer both! You can rent the studio for your own shoots ($100/hr) or book our professional photography services for portraits, events, headshots, and full-day productions.
What's your cancellation policy for studio bookings? +
Cancellations 48+ hours before your booking receive a full refund. 24-48 hours: 50% refund. Less than 24 hours: no refund.
How far in advance should I book a photo session? +
For standard sessions, we recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance. For weddings and large events, booking 2-3 months ahead ensures availability. However, we often accommodate last-minute bookings when possible.

Airbnb Stays

5 Questions
Where is your Airbnb property located? +
Our modern townhouse is located in Metro on Main, Morton Grove, IL. It's a 5-minute walk to the Morton Grove Metra station and approximately 20 minutes from O'Hare Airport.
How many guests can the property accommodate? +
The private room sleeps up to 2 guests comfortably. It's perfect for solo travelers, couples, or business professionals.
What amenities are included? +
Fast Wi-Fi, comfortable bed with fresh linens, heating & A/C, keyless entry, shared common areas, and access to a newly built modern home with 800 thread count bedding.
Is parking available? +
Yes! Free parking is available right in front of the townhouse. There's always plenty of street parking in the Metro on Main community.
What's the check-in process? +
Check-in is after 3:00 PM and check-out is before 11:00 AM. We use keyless entry for your convenience - you'll receive a code before arrival. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request.
