Frequently Asked Questions

Is parking available? +
Yes! Free street parking is available in the neighborhood. The area is residential with ample parking spots typically available.
How does self check-in work? +
You'll receive a unique door code before your arrival. Simply enter the code on the keypad for 24/7 access—no need to coordinate schedules or wait for keys.
Are there other guests or shared spaces? +
This is a private room within a townhouse. You'll have your own bedroom with a lock, and share common areas (kitchen, living room, bathroom) with the host or potentially other guests.
What's the WiFi speed? +
High-speed WiFi with 150+ Mbps download speeds—perfect for video calls, streaming, and remote work. The network name and password are provided in your welcome message.
Are pets allowed? +
Unfortunately, we cannot accommodate pets at this time. Please contact us if you have questions about service animals.
How far is the Metra station? +
The Morton Grove Metra station is just a 5-minute walk from the property. From there, you can reach downtown Chicago in about 35 minutes.
