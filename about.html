Our Beginning

Our Story

Saint Core Holdings was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that excellence shouldn't be fragmented. Whether you're stepping into a photography studio, checking into a curated Airbnb, or hiring someone to mount your TV, you deserve the same level of professionalism, attention to detail, and care.

Based in Chicago with operations across the greater metropolitan area, we've built our reputation by doing what others said couldn't be done—creating a unified ecosystem of premium services that actually work together. From our 2,500 sq ft photography studio in the heart of the city to our luxury short-term rentals in Morton Grove's Metro on Main community, every touchpoint carries the Saint Core seal of quality.

We're not just a photography company, a property management firm, or a handyman service. We're a holdings company that understands modern life requires modern solutions. Your photographer today might maintain your Airbnb tomorrow. Your handyman might prep your studio next week. That's the Saint Core difference—vertical integration that ensures quality at every level.
